# Privacy Policy for Trench Chat

**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026  
**Effective Date:** February 16, 2026

## Introduction

Trench Chat ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the Trench Chat Chrome Extension (the "Extension").

By using Trench Chat, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you register for Trench Chat, we collect:

- **Username** (required): Used to identify you in chat rooms and on leaderboards
- **Password** (required): Encrypted and stored securely for account authentication
- **Email Address** (optional): Used for account recovery and important notifications
- **Referral Code** (optional): If you were referred by another user
- **Profile Information** (optional):
  - Bio/Description
  - Avatar/Profile Picture
  - Solana (SOL) Wallet Address

### 1.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you use Trench Chat, we automatically collect:

- **Chat Messages**: All messages you send in chat rooms are stored on our servers
- **Room Participation**: Which chat rooms (Contract Addresses) you join
- **Usage Data**:
  - Number of messages sent
  - Rooms joined
  - Points earned
  - Streak data (daily check-ins)
  - Referral statistics
- **Reactions**: Emoji reactions you add to messages
- **Images**: Any images you share in chat rooms
- **Authentication Tokens**: JWT tokens used to keep you logged in (stored locally in your browser)

### 1.3 Clipboard Data

The Extension requests permission to read your clipboard **only** when you explicitly click the "Quick Chat" button. This feature:

- Reads the clipboard to detect if you have copied a Contract Address
- Automatically joins the corresponding chat room
- **Does NOT** continuously monitor your clipboard
- **Does NOT** store clipboard contents
- **Does NOT** transmit clipboard data except the Contract Address needed to join a room

## 3. How We Store Your Information

### 3.1 Data Storage Location
- **Server Provider**: Railway (https://railway.app)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL hosted on Railway's infrastructure
- **Location**: United States (Railway's default region)

### 3.2 Security Measures
- **Passwords**: Hashed using bcrypt (industry-standard encryption)
- **Data Transmission**: All data transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted connections)
- **Authentication**: JWT tokens with 30-day expiration
- **Access Control**: Server-side authentication required for all API requests

### 3.3 Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data stored indefinitely while account is active
- **Chat Messages**: Stored permanently to maintain chat history
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data may be retained for up to 30 days for backup purposes
- **Inactive Accounts**: Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be deleted
